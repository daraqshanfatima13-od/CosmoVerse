🚀 Historic Space Missions

🛰️ Sputnik 1 (1957)

The world's first artificial satellite launched by the Soviet Union. It marked the beginning of the Space Age.

👨‍🚀 Yuri Gagarin (1961)

Yuri Gagarin became the first human to travel into space aboard Vostok 1, completing one orbit around Earth.

🌕 Apollo 11 (1969)

Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin became the first humans to walk on the Moon while Michael Collins remained in lunar orbit.

🚀 Voyager 1 (1977)

Voyager 1 explored Jupiter and Saturn before becoming the first spacecraft to enter interstellar space.

🇮🇳 Chandrayaan-3 (2023)

India became the first nation to successfully land near the Moon's south pole, a historic achievement in lunar exploration.

🪐 Planet Explorer

☿ Mercury

The smallest planet and closest to the Sun. It has extreme temperatures and no atmosphere.

♀ Venus

Known as Earth's twin, but its thick atmosphere makes it the hottest planet.

🌍 Earth

The only known planet to support life, with 71% of its surface covered by water.

♂ Mars

The Red Planet is home to Olympus Mons, the tallest volcano in the Solar System.

♃ Jupiter

The largest planet, famous for its Great Red Spot, a giant storm.

♄ Saturn

Known for its spectacular rings made of ice, dust, and rock.

⛢ Uranus

Uranus rotates on its side, making it unique among all the planets.

♆ Neptune

The farthest planet from the Sun, known for its powerful winds.
